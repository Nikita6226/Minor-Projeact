Basic and Necessary Information

  Maruti Temple of Shirsada village, also known as Shirsada's Hanuman Temple, is a Hindu temple located in the village of Shirsada in Bodwad taluka in Maharashtra's Jalgaon district. Its deity is Hanuman.


  Maruti Temple of Shirsada village, also known as Shirsada's Hanuman Temple, is a Hindu temple located in the village of Shirsada in Bodwad taluka in Maharashtra's Jalgaon district. Its deity is Hanuman.


  The Mandira is unique in the sense that it does not have a Shikhar, Amlak, and Kadas. Several other temples are located in the temple premises... Maruti Temple is situated 7.45 miles away from the town of Muktainagar and 46 Miles from Jalgaon.
